Installing eLua

To use eLua in one of the supported platforms, you need to install it in your target kit/device or, more precisely, to flash the eLua image into the platform of your choice.
This section shows specific installation instructions and tips, for all the CPU/boards supported by eLua.

First, you need to have a suitable eLua image for your target.
You can either download one of our ready to use binary images or build eLua to have custom image for your needs.

Please choose the platform of your interest from the "Platform Info" submenu for more instructions.
